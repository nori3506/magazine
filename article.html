Learn from the Fashion Emperor

NOVEMBER 24, 2020 8:49 AM

In the fall of 2020, Mr. James Thomas, known as the Emperor of the business fashion world, is launching a new trend.
Since his fashion is a bit dissociated from business it's sometimes controversial, but the combination of sophisticated tops and bottoms is praised by the world. While he is a major figure in an IT company in NY, He is a super busy fashion model who appears in 40 fashion shows a year all over the world. Not only that, there are well over 1000 companies and producers trying to approach him, including offers as a singer, movie appearances, and headhunting from top companies.
"How has he built up his career?"
"How can I become a star from the average person like him?"
People would have had such questions many times.
MEN'S NORIS has successfully conducted an exclusive interview with him to approach these answers.
